What To Do If You Smell Gas
  • Switch off the stove / cooker.
  • Turn off the Gas emergency control valve.
  • Ensure that windows and doors are open to allow the gas to dissipate.
  • DO NOT operate any electrical instruments, switches or lights.
  • Call the Emergency Operation and Maintenance (“EOM”) contractor of your building for assistance. The contact details of your EOM contractor must be obtained from your facility/building management and be readily available to you at all times.
  • Call the EOM contractor of ADNOC Distribution at [insert telephone number].
