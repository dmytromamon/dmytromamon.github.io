Why Certree?

About Certree

Just as your secrets are safe with a tree, you can trust your secrets with Certree. In a world where most companies harvest our personal data for their benefit, Certree exists to protect our data privacy and allows each of us to have a secure private vault for our personal documents. By design, nobody at Certree can see the information on our platform.

With Certree’s Employee Protection application, we put employees, each of us, in control of how our personal sensitive information is shared and with whom. Our purpose is to safeguard employee privacy and protect employer brand.
