What is Certree Employee Protection for Employers?

Instead of sending private data to third parties or risk releasing employee data directly to verifiers without employee consent, Certree Employee Protection is designed as a secure location for you to issue your employees’ private information, such as proof of employment/income, directly to them. Certree Employee Protection safeguards your company brand, and it’s free for you and your employees to use.

Became an issuer, It's Free!

Benefits

  • Compliance

    Reduce legal risks associated with privacy laws such as CCPA, FCRA and GDPR.

  • Productivity

    Free up your in-house HR staff. Leverage Certree’s employee self-service feature and have private documents issued directly to your employees with zero touch.

  • Brand

    Protect your brand by putting document distribution in the hands of your employee’s. You issue the trusted documents, they send to verifiers they trust.

  • Trust

    Only you, as the employer, can issue and modify documents.

  • Privacy

    Once the documents are in a Certree account, only your employee and the authorized verifiers the employee selects can view the documents.
