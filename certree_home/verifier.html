What is Certree Employee Protection for Verifiers?

Certree is changing the current employment/income verification process by allowing candidates to share proof of employment/income directly with you. Certree certifies that the documents you view on the platform are tamper-free.

Become a Verifier

Benefits

  • Compliance

    By getting access directly from the candidate, you automatically stay compliant with FCRA.

  • Legal risk

    Accuracy of the data is verified by candidates before they share it with you. You avoid liability for sending inaccurate data about an employee or for hiring a new employee based on incorrect data.

  • Speed

    Instead of weeks, verification takes minutes.

  • Trust

    Only the employer can issue the document. Neither the candidate nor Certree can modify the content.

FAQ

If candidates have a Certree Employee Account, they will send you an access link to their proof of employment/income. You click on the access link to view the document.

Certree has been adopted by many Fortune 500 companies and has passed rigorous security checks. By design, only employers can issue or modify proof of employment/income on the Certree platform. Neither the employee nor Certree can modify the content so documents are certified tamper-free.

When you receive an access link, you may choose to have one-time access to the document. If you have frequent verifications, a Verifier account on Certree will help you manage your responsibilities and cost better.
