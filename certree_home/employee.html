What is Certree Employee Protection for Employees?

Certree is intended for employees to have your personal documents such as proof of income and proof of employment issued directly to your Certree account. You can use the self service option to request documents when needed. If your employer has directed you here, please sign up for a Certree account and follow instructions to request your documents. Remember, it is your responsibility to review your documents’ accuracy and share the access link for your documents with verifiers who request them.

Sign up to request your documents

Benefits

  • Control

    You control who can access your personal information, the specific information that is shared with verifiers and for how long they have access.

  • Transaprency

    You can verify the accuracy of your data before sharing with verifiers. No more surprises or missed opportunities with incorrect information being passed around about you without your awareness.

  • Privacy

    Only your employer, you, and verifiers that you authorize can access your data.

  • Speed

    Get your proof of employment or proof of income in minutes instead of weeks and speed up the process to get a loan or a new job.

  • Protection

    Certree is designed to protect individual’s data so there is no risk of a mass data breach and your personal information being exposed.

FAQ

Step 1: Sign up for a Certree account (or sign in) and follow the instructions to get your Proof of Employment/Income.

Step 2: Review your documents to verify the accuracy. If there are discrepancies, contact your employer to correct them.

Step 3: Share the access link with the verifier (may be a loan company, bank, background check company, future employer, landlord, etc.) who requests your documents.

The documents that verifiers access via Certree are Certree certified. Our technology guarantees that only your employer can modify the documents on the Certree platform.

If they are an issuer with Certree, request your documents directly from your Certree account.

If they are not an issuer with Certree, contact the HR departments of your previous employers and request that they sign up for a free employer account on the Certree platform.

By design, Certree is unable to see your documents. Please contact your employer to correct any mistakes. Your employer can re-issue a corrected document to your account.
