Biography

Madeline Kelly is a film & commercial director. Born in Sydney, she lives in New York City. Seeking to represent voices seldom portrayed on screen, Madeline’s body of work sums her up as a filmmaker with an acute sensitivity to the human experience. She’s drawn to intimate stories told with energy and emotion.

Her short films have screened at prestigious film festivals around the world (Palm Springs, Iris Prize) and gone on to broadcast on National television. Commercially she’s worked with a range of international clients including Hanes, Unilever, Viacom, Clemmenger & Ogilvy.
