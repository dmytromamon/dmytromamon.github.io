About Teacher Training

This is a unique and innovative training on how an English platform (technology) can assist our teachers to use active learning skills to achieve greater learning outcomes in their schools. The key to achieving greater outcomes is to overcome the many challenges our teachers face today:

  • Limited resources
  • Overcrowded classrooms
  • Varying English proficiencies among students
  • Large workloads for teachers
  • Limited learning environment

Benefits for school

  • Transform the role of the teachers to coach students through active learning (a method of learning in which students are actively or experientially involved in the learning process and where there are different levels of active learning, depending on student involvement.)
  • Whoever wishes to learn can be taught - no discrimination between students
  • Teachers can make personalized learning plans for each student
  • Substantially lower workloads for teachers
