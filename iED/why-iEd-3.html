About us

What is the immersive education academy?

English is the global language that shapes how we communicate, interact and think. At the Immersive Education Academy (iED for short), we guide and inspire our students to learn English fluently by teaching with immerse, interactive, real-world experiences. Using technology, you can learn remotely, crafting a unique learning experience that is interactive, fun, and collaborative. Our Academy allows you to take complete control of your education with four main tools. We have the iED Classroom, iED Online, iED Café, and the Immersion Field Trip. Each gives students the opportunity to personalize their education according to their goals.

Our sophisticated platform also gives you the opportunity to accelerate your learning by offering customized lessons so you can use the materials that best match your learning style. You can track your progress, try quizzes, watch videos or access English recordings.

We hire world-class instructors from across the globe to ensure you get the best education available. Our courses are tailor-made for our students, so you get individualized teaching that fits your needs and learning style.

Our goals

Become fluent in English with an engaging multimedia our learning platform

Our core program is the Academic Program, which is specifically designed for students who want to learn conversational English to help them communicate in their daily lives. This course goes beyond just surface-level interaction and teaches you to express your ideas, persuade others, and interpret English ques – all deep level communication skills needed to succeed in a global market.

Our flexibility and technology provides our students with an entirely new way of learning English. No longer are you tied to an uncomfortable desk seat. You can learn at home on your computer or on the go with your smartphone. There are also other customized programs that students can enroll in for people of different ages designed for your individual needs and goals.

Our mission

The easiest way to learn English anywhere in the world

As an advanced technology educator, our focus is on making the entire learning process easy, fun, and comprehensive so you excel in your studies. This allows you further opportunities in post secondary and your career beyond your education.

Our teachers use a wide range of teaching materials, lesson plans, and methodologies that we adapt to each student and class. In addition to our classroom sessions, we have interactive online portals including iED Online and iED Cafe where you can practice what you’ve learned and further your knowledge. Lastly the Immersion Field Trip is a fun and exciting opportunity to practice your English while learning about different locations and cultures.

Professor Tak Wah Mak is an immunologist and cancer researcher. His landmark paper on the cloning of a human T cell receptor gene was an important breakthrough in our understanding of the human immune system. His current research interests include the biology of the immune system, the biology of programmed cell death and the pathogenesis of cancer. Recently, he has isolated and analysed the effects of genes that regulate the immune system and suppress tumours, paving the way for future therapies.

Dr. Mak is a Professor in the Departments of Medical Biophysics and Immunology at the University of Toronto; and Director of the Campbell Family Institute for Breast Cancer Research. His achievements have been recognised through international awards, including the Gairdner International Prize, the Novartis Prize in Immunology, the Sloan Prize, the Paul Ehrlich Prize and the King Faisal Prize of Medicine. Dr. Mak is a foreign associate of the US National Academy of Sciences and a Fellow of the Royal Society. He took up the chairmanship of the Croucher Foundation in June 2011.
