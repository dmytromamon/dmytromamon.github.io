Partner High School Program (PHS)

Provide learning opportunities for students that will help them prepare for tertiary education and allow students to experience a top University education and earn credits early.

  • Length: 9 months
  • Courses: 28
  • Institution: University of Illinous at Urbana Champaign
  • Level: Advanced
  • Language: English

About PHS Program

The Partner High School Program is designed with local high schools in mind. It is a way for schools and students to have access to quality education programs from the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. Through a blended learning model, university courses are taught at local high schools for certificate or credit. These credits can be used towards admissions to the University of Illinois or can be transferred around the world.

Benefits for school

  • Connect your brand with the University of Illinois
  • University certified teachers at your school
  • Provide freshman and sophomore courses in Vietnam during or after high school
  • Enable parents to save money on university tuition
  • Enable students to graduate from their undergraduate degree earlier when studying overseas

Benefits for students

  • Experience education from a top 50 globally ranked University before studying overseas
  • Take university courses in Vietnam during or after high school and earn university credits
  • Earn credits and graduate faster
  • Use course credits for admissions to UIUC or other top universities
  • Save money on university tuition

The University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign

Background

The University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign is one of the original 37 public land-grant institutions created after President Abraham Lincoln signed the Morrill Act in 1862.

The university is made of 17 colleges and offers more than 150 programs of study. As of 2016, the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign alumni, faculty, and researchers include 28 Nobel laureates, and 22 Pulitzer Prize recipients.

University rankings compiled by U.S News & World Report, Times Higher Education and the Academic Ranking of World Universities rank Illinois among the top 50 universities in the world, and one of the top 5 engineering schools in the world.

Resources and Achievements

Academic resources on campus are among the finest in the world. The university library is one of the largest public university collections in the world with 13 million volumes in its 37 unit libraries. Annually, 53,000,000 people visit its online catalog. Students have access to thousands of computer in terminals in classrooms, residence halls, and campus libraries for use in classroom instruction, study, and research.

Courses in this Program

The courses offered through the Partner High School Program are considered the same as the courses offered on campus at the university. Students receive the same university credit and transcripts for completing the courses.

Furthermore, the courses offered through the Partner High School Program meet the General Educatoin requirements at the University of Illinois and many universities in the United States, making them highly transferrable.
