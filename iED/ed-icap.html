Illinois Composite Admissions Program (ICAP)

Enables high schools to guarantee the admissions of their students into Arizona State University’s degree programs. The Prerequisite for the guaranteed admissions is that the students must take the IEd English Program before graduating high school during grades 10-12.

  • Length: 3 months
  • Courses: 6-8
  • Institution: University of Illinous at Urbana Champaign
  • Level: Advanced
  • Language: English

Benefits for school

  • Schools that join this program will become partners of Arizona State University and their high school
  • Accredited by AdvancED (www.advanc-ed.org)
  • Same accreditation used by famous schools in China (Ex. Shanghai World Foreign Language Academy)

Benefits for students

  • Students earn 18 freshman credits. These credits are transferrable to other universities
  • Students can earn an accredited US High School diploma from Arizona State University’s high school
  • Students who receive the US diploma will be entitled to admissions at ASU.
  • Students enrolled will be assigned to an academic counsellor. This is an added benefit and support personnel to help you successfully complete courses. The counsellor will also help you with tracking your path to a U.S. university
